About Us

Hasbiyallah Charity Organization Uganda is an Islamic based non-profit organization that was founded in 2023. Our aim is to extend help to the needy without discrimination in terms of religion, gender and age

Our Mission:

At Hasbiyallah Charity Organization, our mission is to provide essential support and resources to the underprivileged and vulnerable members of our community. We believe in the power of compassion, generosity, and unity to make a difference in the lives of those who need it most.

What We Do:

  • Humanitarian Aid: We provide food, clothing, and shelter to families in crisis.
  • Education Support: Our programs include scholarships, school supplies, and educational resources for children and adults.
  • Healthcare Services: We offer medical camps, health screenings, and access to essential medications.
  • Community Development: We focus on sustainable projects such as clean water initiatives, building infrastructure, and skill development programs.
